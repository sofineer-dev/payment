About Us

Time Travelers Logistics

Based in Augusta, South Carolina, Time Travelers Logistics specializes in tailored car shipping services for car dealerships. We provide both Open Transport and Enclosed Transport options, facilitating Dealer Trades, Auction-to-Dealer, Dealer-to-Auction, and home delivery for dealership customers within a 100-mile radius. Our mission is to optimize dealership operations by reducing downtime, speeding up delivery times, and increasing inventory turnover rates to drive higher sales for car dealerships.
