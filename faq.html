Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Welcome to the Time Travelers Logistics FAQ page. Here, you'll find answers to some of the most common questions about our auction-to-dealer and dealer-to-auction vehicle transport services.

  • 1 What services does Time Travelers Logistics offer?

    We specialize in auction-to-dealer and dealer-to-auction vehicle transport across the lower 48 states. Our services include:

    • Auction-to-Dealer Transport
    • Dealer-to-Auction Transport
    • Dealer Trade Transport
    • Home Deliveries for Dealerships
  • 2 Which areas do you service?

    We provide nationwide auto transport services, with a strong focus on:

    • Augusta, GA
    • North Augusta, SC
    • Charleston, SC
    • Columbia, SC
    • Nashville, TN
    • Atlanta, GA
    • Baton Rouge, LA
    • Nashville, TN
  • 3 Can you guarantee that my price won't change?

    Yes, we guarantee our pricing unless there are changes or inaccuracies in the information provided during the quoting process. Any discrepancies may necessitate a pricing review.

  • 4 How do I get a quote for vehicle transport?

    Click here to get a quote Instant Quote or please contact us at (803) 806-9830 or email AnaBoyd@TimeTravelersLogistics.com.

  • 5 Is there a discount for shipping multiple vehicles?

    Yes, we offer discounts for shipping multiple vehicles. The discount depends on factors such as weight, size, distance, and the cities involved in the pick-up or drop-off.

  • 6 Why is a shorter trip sometimes more expensive than a longer trip?

    A shorter trip can be more expensive if the pick-up or drop-off location is far from other loads. This results in "deadhead" or empty trailer miles, which increases operating costs.

  • 7 Why is your cost higher than the competition?

    We provide accurate pricing to guarantee prompt vehicle pick-up and delivery. Unlike competitors who may offer standby rates that can delay transportation, our priority is ensuring timely delivery. This approach is particularly advantageous for dealers, as it reduces downtime and accelerates turnover rates. Opting for the lowest price does not always ensure reliable and prompt service.

  • 8 What is Auction-to-Dealer Transport?

    Auction-to-Dealer Transport involves moving vehicles from auction locations to dealerships. We ensure that your vehicles are transported safely and efficiently, minimizing downtime and maximizing inventory turnover.

  • 9 What is Dealer-to-Auction Transport?

    Dealer-to-Auction Transport involves transporting vehicles from dealerships to auction sites. Our service is designed to be reliable and efficient, ensuring your vehicles arrive on time and in excellent condition.

  • 10 What is Dealer Trade Transport?

    Dealer Trade Transport manages the transportation of vehicles between dealerships over long distances. We handle the logistics to ensure a smooth and timely transfer of vehicles, enhancing your dealership's inventory management.

  • 11 Do you offer home delivery services for dealerships?

    Yes, we offer home deliveries for customers located over 100 miles away from the dealership. This service enhances customer satisfaction by providing a convenient delivery option.

  • 12 How reliable is your service?

    We provide services 7 days a week and handle jobs of any size with utmost reliability. Our commitment to being the fastest transport company ensures timely deliveries, helping you maintain optimal inventory levels.

  • 13 Is My Vehicle Covered by Insurance During Transport?

    When you entrust Time Travelers Logistics with shipping your vehicle, you can be confident that the carrier assigned for transportation is fully insured and licensed by the FMCSA (Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration). Your vehicle is protected under the carrier’s insurance policy, which varies in coverage from $100,000 to $1,000,000 depending on the type and size of the trailer used for transport.

  • 14 Can I transport personal items in my vehicle?

    We advise against transporting personal belongings in your vehicle during transport. Auto transport insurance does not cover loss or damage to personal items left in the vehicle. Additionally, if your car is packed with belongings, the driver may refuse to transport it.
